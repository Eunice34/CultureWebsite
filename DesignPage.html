Interior Design Trends

Let's get green

We wish we owned ten million house plants, but, until then, we will settle for looking at ten million pictures of plants on Instagram. These are our favorite accounts to scroll though when we're looking for interior decorating tips with plants and/or photos of attractive people and cute dogs next to Monstera leaves.

  1. HOUSEOFPLANTS

    2. We never thought to put a giant Rubber plant on a cabinet, but we are honestly very into the idea.

  2. THESILL

    3. The Instagram for this famous plant shop is rainforest of delights, especially this Iris Apfel photo (who we are huge fans of).

  3. EARTHWINDANDCACTUS

    4. This Instagram devoted to cacti is sunny adventure on demand. The mirror's not bad either.

  4. PLANTMAMA_

    5. We love a green wall and would be happy to be adopted by this plant mama.

  5. HOUSEPLANTCLUB

    6. Honestly, the only club we are interested in joining/going to.

  6. THEPOTTEDJUNGLE

    7. House plants and rattan furniture are a match made in heaven.

  7. SARAKBENNING

    8. If you can't trust yourself to keep a plant alive, you can buy these beautiful embroideries instead.

  8. SUCCULENT CITY

    9. Take me down to succulent city where the plants are green and the bedrooms are pretty.

  9. HILTONCARTER

    10. Hilton Carter's loft is filled with more house plants than we can count, not that we'd refuse an invitation to come do so.

  10. WILDERNISAMSTERDAM

    11. Amsterdam is a great place to investigate leaves, like at this uber-chic botanical workshop.

  11. JAMIES_JUNGLE

    12. This account posts a spread of plant-filled interiors that make us want to pack our bags and move somewhere with high ceilings and better windows.

  12. FOLIA_FOLIA

    13. Truly, #shelfgoals.
